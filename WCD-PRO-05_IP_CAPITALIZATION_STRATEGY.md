## VECTOR 1: NON-TRADITIONAL IP FINANCING

### 1A. Revenue-Based Financing (RBF)

**Mechanism:** Borrow against future recurring revenue. Repayment is a fixed percentage (4-8%) of monthly revenue until a cap (1.3x-2x) is reached. No equity dilution.

**Eligibility Threshold:**
- Minimum $10K MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue)
- 6+ months revenue history
- 60%+ gross margins

**Lenders to Approach:**
| Lender | Check Size | Factor | Repayment |
|--------|-----------|--------|-----------|
| **Pipe** | $50K-$5M | 3-5x MRR | Revenue share |
| **Capchase** | $100K-$5M | 4-6x MRR | Weekly/monthly |
| **Lighter Capital** | $50K-$4M | 1.2-1.5x cap | 2-4% monthly |
| **Clearco** | $10K-$10M | 2-4x MRR | Revenue share |

**Legal Instrument:** Revenue Participation Agreement (RPA) — NOT a loan. Structured as "future revenue purchase."

**Action:** Once Hosted API hits $10K MRR, apply to Pipe or Capchase. Use capital to scale infrastructure.

### 1B. Trademark/Brand IP Collateralization

**Mechanism:** Borrow against the registered trademark and brand value, not the code.

**Process:**
1. File USPTO trademark application for "P31" and "Cognitive Prosthetic" ($250-350 per class)
2. Obtain brand valuation via **IPWE** or **Ocean Tomo** ($5K-15K)
3. Use valuation as collateral for SBA 7(a) loan or credit union line of credit

**Lenders:**
| Institution | Program | Collateral |
|-------------|---------|------------|
| **Live Oak Bank** | SBA 7(a) | IP/Trademark |
| **Wells Fargo** | Innovation Lending | IP Portfolio |

**Legal Instrument:** Security Agreement with IP Collateral Schedule (UCC-1 filing).

### 1C. Future Enterprise Contract Factoring

**Mechanism:** Sell signed enterprise contracts (POs) at a discount for immediate cash.

**Process:**
1. Sign 12-month enterprise contract ($50K-$200K ARR)
2. Submit to factoring company
3. Receive 80-90% upfront, remainder minus fee upon collection

**Factors:**
| Factor | Advance Rate | Fee |
|--------|-------------|-----|
| **Pipe** | 85-90% | 3-5% |
| **Capchase** | 80-90% | 2-4% |

**Legal Instrument:** Purchase Order Factoring Agreement.

## VECTOR 2: OPEN-SOURCE MONETIZATION MODELS (B2B)

### 2A. Dual-Licensing

**Structure:**
- **AGPLv3** for individuals/non-commercial (code must remain open if modified and distributed)
- **Commercial License** for institutions/clinics who:
  - Cannot use AGPL (proprietary integration)
  - Need warranty/indemnification
  - Require SSO/enterprise support

**Pricing Model:**
| Tier | Price | Includes |
|------|-------|----------|
| **Individual** | Free (AGPL) | Source code, community support |
| **Clinical/Institutional** | $5,000-25,000/yr | Commercial license, indemnification, SLA |
| **Enterprise** | $50,000-150,000/yr | White-label, custom integration, dedicated support |

**Precedent:** MySQL ($1B+ revenue via dual-licensing), GitLab (open-core + enterprise).

**Legal Instrument:** Commercial Software License Agreement (CSLA) with per-seat or per-deployment terms.

**Time to Revenue:** 60-90 days (requires legal review + sales outreach).

### 2B. Hosted API / SaaS (RECOMMENDED)

**Structure:**
- **Free tier:** Self-hosted, local PGLite (current)
- **Paid tier:** Hosted 7-node mesh, managed infrastructure, $20-50/mo

**Technical Requirements:**
- Deploy mesh to AWS/GCP/DigitalOcean
- Stripe integration for billing
- Multi-tenant isolation

**Pricing:**
| Plan | Price | Features |
|------|-------|----------|
| **Hobby** | Free | Self-hosted, community support |
| **Pro** | $20/mo | Hosted mesh, 10K API calls, email support |
| **Team** | $99/mo | 5 users, 100K calls, SLA |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Dedicated infra, SSO, compliance |

**Revenue Projection (Conservative):**
- Month 3: 100 users × $20 = $2K MRR
- Month 6: 500 users × $20 = $10K MRR
- Month 12: 2,000 users × $30 avg = $60K MRR

**Legal Instrument:** SaaS Subscription Agreement with Data Processing Addendum (DPA) for HIPAA compliance.

**Time to Revenue:** 30-45 days (Stripe + deployment).
