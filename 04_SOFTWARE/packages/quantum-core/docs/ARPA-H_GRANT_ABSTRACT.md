# ARPA-H Grant Abstract

**Project Title:** Quantum-Symmetric Agentic Swarms for Rare Endocrine Disorder Management and Post-Quantum Medical Device Security

**Target Area:** Health Science Futures / Resilient Systems

**Principal Investigator:** Will Johnson, P31 Labs

---

## 1. Innovation and Impact

Current chronic disease management applications rely on monolithic classical AI models that suffer from hallucination, overlapping data bias, and cognitive overload for neurodivergent patients. Furthermore, the telemetry generated by wearable Internet of Medical Things (IoMT) devices is currently secured by RSA/ECC cryptography, making highly sensitive longitudinal biological data vulnerable to "Store Now, Decrypt Later" (SNDL) quantum attacks.

**P31 Labs proposes a paradigm shift: Quantum-Symmetric Agentic Healthcare.**

By utilizing Symmetric Informationally Complete Positive Operator-Valued Measures (SIC-POVM), we map biological homeostasis (specifically addressing Hypoparathyroidism) to a mathematical quantum state ($d$-dimensional Hilbert space). A coordinated swarm of exactly $d^2$ AI agents performs biological state tomography, guaranteeing perfectly symmetric, non-overlapping data analysis.

---

## 2. Technical Methodology

### SIC-POVM Biological Tomography
The $d^2$ swarm processes continuous wearable telemetry (HRV, temperature) and manual logs to predict life-threatening calcium crashes. Each agent represents one POVM element with weight $1/d$, ensuring mathematical symmetry in state reconstruction.

### QAOA Dosing Optimization
Implementation of the Quantum Approximate Optimization Algorithm to calculate highly specific, multi-variable dosing schedules for calcium carbonate and calcitriol. The cost function minimizes calcium fluctuation around the homeostasis target.

### FIPS 203/204 Post-Quantum Security
All transport layers and patient Decentralized Identifiers (W3C DIDs) are secured at the edge (Cloudflare Workers) using NIST-validated ML-KEM and ML-DSA cryptography. This provides defense-in-depth against SNDL attacks.

### Cognitive Accessibility
Enforcing "Collapse Directives" to translate high-dimensional mathematical data into zero-friction "Traffic Light" UX paradigms, specifically engineered to bypass executive dysfunction in AuDHD patients during physiological crises.

## 3. Anticipated Outcomes

A medical-grade, quantum-secured edge infrastructure capable of predicting rare-disease physiological crashes with >98% confidence, while establishing the future standard for FDA cybersecurity compliance in post-quantum medical devices.
