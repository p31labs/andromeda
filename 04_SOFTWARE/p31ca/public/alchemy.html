01. The Principle of Independent Convergence

The P31 thesis rests on a verifiable anomaly: three completely independent scientific fields — quantum measurement theory, molecular biophysics, and network topology — independently converge on the same geometric optimum: tetrahedral symmetry. This is not metaphor. It is structural necessity.

02. Applications

The Double Empathy Problem → Circuit Model

Milton (2012) describes mutual misattribution between neurotypes as a bidirectional failure — not a deficit in the autistic party. The P31 model maps this to electrical circuit theory: a Wye (Y) topology with a floating neutral. When the neutral node is removed (social exclusion, institutional betrayal), voltage collapses at the remaining endpoints. Converting to a Delta (Δ) topology — the K₄ tetrahedron — eliminates the floating neutral entirely. There is no single point of failure.

Impedance Matching as Social Thermodynamics

Maximum power transfer in an electrical circuit occurs when source and load impedances are matched. P31 models social friction as impedance mismatch between neurotypes operating on different sensory-processing baselines. BONDING's game mechanics implement impedance matching: the chemistry of bonding (covalent, ionic, metallic) maps directly to human relationship formation strategies.

The Faraday Cage for the Mind

A Faraday cage excludes external electromagnetic fields by distributing induced charges across its conductive surface. The hardware (Node One), software (The Buffer), digital (Kenosis), and legal (Miller Shield) layers form the four faces of the cage.
