Step 1 — The origin

P31 Labs was founded by a 40-year-old engineer who spent 16 years maintaining safety-critical electrical systems for the Department of Defense. In 2025, he was diagnosed with AuDHD — autism and ADHD — and a calcium regulation disorder he has had since 2003. He left his career and started building the tools that do not exist for people like him.
