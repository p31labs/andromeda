Commitment

P31 Labs builds neuro-inclusive flows (e.g. planetary onboard) and aims for WCAG-aligned contrast and keyboard-friendly controls on new static deliverables. Legacy demos may not yet meet the same bar.

Report a barrier

Email will@p31ca.org with the page URL, browser, and what blocked you (screen reader, keyboard, contrast, motion). We cannot guarantee timelines but we track operator-facing fixes.
