Telemetry Posture

Edition 1.0.0 · Effective 2026-05-02 · Companion to CWP-P31-PEER-COMP-2026-05. Canonical source: docs/TELEMETRY.md.

The one-line answer

P31 collects no client-side telemetry. Period. CI fails if any is added without a documented exception.

What this means concretely

No Google Analytics. No Google Tag Manager. No Plausible, Fathom, Simple Analytics, Umami, GoatCounter. No Mixpanel, Amplitude, Segment, PostHog, Heap. No Sentry, Bugsnag, Rollbar, Datadog browser RUM, New Relic browser. No Hotjar, FullStory, LogRocket, Microsoft Clarity, Smartlook. No Facebook Pixel, TikTok Pixel, Pinterest Tag, LinkedIn Insight, Twitter Analytics. No Cloudflare Web Analytics. No A/B testing platforms. No session replay tools. No advertising pixels of any kind.

The full deny list is enforced by scripts/verify-no-telemetry.mjs · updated when a new vendor pattern needs to be added.

What this does NOT mean

  • Cloudflare Pages and Cloudflare Workers retain their own access logs under Cloudflare's policy. We do not separately collect or analyze them.
  • Browsers retain their own histories. That is the user's browser, not ours.
  • The Cognitive Passport stays in localStorage on the user's device and is read by hub pages there. The hub never transmits the passport to a P31 server.
  • Stripe Checkout runs on Stripe's domain when a user clicks “donate”; Stripe collects what Stripe collects per its own policy.
  • The local command center (http://127.0.0.1:3131, operator-only) writes audit logs to ~/.p31/operator-shift.jsonl. That is on the operator's machine, not transmitted off-device.
  • The internal P31 ecosystem dashboard at /telemetry is an operator-facing visualization of the local mesh state, not user telemetry. It does not collect or transmit anything from public visitors.

Why no telemetry

  1. The operator is the product. Behavior surveillance of an operator with hypoparathyroidism, AuDHD, and an active civil case would be hostile.
  2. The K₄ family mesh holds children. Surveillance of their navigation patterns would be a betrayal even if the data never left our servers.
  3. The public hub serves cold strangers. Anyone walking in deserves the same treatment we give the operator.
  4. Telemetry is the foundation of engagement-maximization. P31's voice doctrine bans the patterns; you cannot ban the patterns and collect the data that feeds them.
  5. Transparency reports are easier when the answer is “we don't have it.” See /transparency.

Reporting a violation

If you find P31 collecting telemetry that is not on our exception list, please report it via docs/security/REPORTING.md. We treat undisclosed telemetry the same as an undisclosed security defect.
