This education area is a progressive front door: read-only first, then catalog search, then short labs that point to real hub tools (connect, Geodesic, doc library) without making the static site pretend to be an LMS.

Who it is for: operators who ship on Cloudflare, architects who need an honest “what runs where” map, and grant or partner reviewers who want a security-aligned narrative tied to the same verify bar as the rest of the P31 home + p31ca monorepo path.

What it is not: a place to paste API keys, cage tokens, or payment material. Donor and MAP flows stay on the existing monetary surfaces. Youth-facing coaching stays in the personal mesh and local-first tools described in PLAN-KIDS-VIBE-CODING — not in anonymous public chat.

Machine index (JSON) lists tracks, modules, and lab pages for CI. The human plan lives in the P31 home repo: docs/PLAN-P31-LABS-EDUCATION-SITE.md.
