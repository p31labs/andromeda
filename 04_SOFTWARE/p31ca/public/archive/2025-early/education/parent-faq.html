Accounts

On E0–E2 there is no sign-in for this education site. Labs use local storage or read-only public tools. Any future portal (E3) would need its own policy review before youth use.

Data

These pages do not collect learner PII in forms. If you use hub tools (passport generator, doc library) separately, see Privacy and product-specific copy.

Youth & COPPA

Flows that could apply to children under 13 in the U.S. require a deliberate legal and product review. This static curriculum does not replace that work. The household coding plan in the home repo is the starting point for youth-facing engineering.
