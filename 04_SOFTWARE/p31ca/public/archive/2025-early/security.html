Security

P31 Labs, Inc. security policy. Last updated 2026-05-02.

Report a vulnerability

Preferred: open a private security advisory:

  • github.com/p31labs/bonding-soup/security/advisories/new
  • github.com/p31labs/andromeda/security/advisories/new

Email: until security@p31labs.org is live (target: end of Q3 2026), email will@p31ca.org with subject [security].

Acknowledgment: within 72 hours. Triage decision: within 7 days. Full process at docs/security/REPORTING.md.

Safe harbor

If you follow the guidelines in our reporting policy, we treat your research as authorized. We will not pursue legal action and we will defend your right to do this work in good faith. Same posture as the Disclose.io common framework.

Public advisories

Every security issue that produces a fix gets a public advisory at docs/security/advisories/. Identifier scheme: P31SA-YYYY-NNN.

  • P31SA-2026-001 — Ecosystem hardening pass (Low; defense-in-depth, no reachable defect). 2026-05-02.

Hall of Fame

Reporters are credited at docs/security/HALL-OF-FAME.md. We do not yet have funds for a paid bug bounty; recognition on the Hall of Fame and a real, dated public advisory are the recognition path until grants land.

Independent audit

Phase 2 of the peer-comparison CWP commits to commissioning an independent security audit (Trail of Bits, NCC Group, Cure53, Doyensec, or Open Privacy Research) and publishing the report in full. The RFP template is public at docs/security/audit-rfp-template.md; engagement is funding-gated.
