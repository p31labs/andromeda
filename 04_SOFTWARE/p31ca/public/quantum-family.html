🐝 Pollination Courier

            
A kind reflex game: you steer a tiny flower-boat and send sparkly nectar puffs. Buds bloom, bees get a gold hello, pods need three soft knocks. You can’t lose — if the cloud strip fills, the sky just asks everyone to take a breath together.

            
Why operators (and skeptical platforms) should care: zero network calls, copyable session manifest for audits, no retention dark patterns, explicit reduced-motion path, keyboard surface — the kind of “attention ethics in a file” product councils ask for in slide decks.

            
Slide or drag to move · Tap for a puff · Space works too · Focus the garden (click outline) then arrows + space · Chill slows drift

            
For a single grown-up JSON bundle (garden + optional play context): open  and enable Include Courier play session.

                ▸ Design charter (auditable)
                
    
                    
  • Non-extractive engagement. No streak punishment, no surprise dark patterns, no “lose” screen — the only hard stop is an explicit calm break that reads as care, not failure.
    • 
                    
  • Local-first evidence. This build does not phone home. The session manifest is JSON you copy yourself — suitable for IRB packets, trust & safety review, or parent transparency.
    • 
                    
  • Inclusion defaults. Keyboard surface, screen-reader milestones, reduced-motion particle caps — the boring compliance layer done without making kids wait for “accessibility mode.”
    • 
                    
  • Child-directed shape. Copy and metaphors are parent-auditable; mechanics reward helping, not dominating.
