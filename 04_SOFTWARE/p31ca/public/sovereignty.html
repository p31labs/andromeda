Simple Sovereignty

Sovereign Stack Home Portal

01. What It Is

Simple Sovereignty is the home screen for the sovereign stack — a single portal that surfaces every P31 tool without requiring you to remember URLs, navigate the Hub, or maintain browser tabs.

The philosophy is literal: sovereignty over your digital environment means one entry point you control, not a browser history of scattered apps. Open one URL. Everything else is one tap away.

02. Portal Grid

Six launch targets organized by function. Each tile shows the tool name, a one-line description, and navigates directly to the app on tap.

03. Terminal Manifest

Below the portal grid is a live terminal that shows the sovereignty stack manifest — which tools are loaded, their version strings, and connection status. The terminal is read-only. It is a status display, not an input surface.

$ sovereignty --manifest
