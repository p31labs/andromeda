What your money buys

  • The 501(c)(3) determination filing fee with the IRS (Form 1023-EZ user fee, $275)
  • Georgia annual nonprofit registration ($30/year)
  • Domain renewals (~6 domains, ~$90/year total)
  • Cloudflare Workers Paid plan when free-tier limits arrive (~$5/month)
  • ESP32 boards, displays, and batteries for kids’ Node Zero builds (~$30 per kit)
  • Print runs of business cards, stickers, and elevator cards for the meatspace bridge (~$0.10 per sheet)

We don’t pay salaries. The operator (William Johnson) builds this on disability income while pro se in active litigation. Every dollar is line-item visible in the public ledger.
