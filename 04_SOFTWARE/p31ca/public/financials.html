Financials

P31 Labs, Inc. · EIN 42-1888158 · Georgia nonprofit (501(c)(3) application pending) · Last updated 2026-05-02.

Where we stand today

P31 Labs incorporated in Georgia on April 3, 2026, and filed for 501(c)(3) recognition shortly after. The IRS determination letter has not yet been issued. Until it is, donations are not yet tax-deductible at the federal level; the Stripe Checkout flow at donate-api.phosphorus31.org processes gifts and queues IRS-conditional receipts.

We will publish IRS Form 990 annually as required. The first 990 will cover the period from incorporation forward and will appear on this page within 30 days of filing. We borrow this commitment language from Signal Foundation's transparency posture.

Donor mechanics

  • No advertising on any P31 surface. Ever. This is doctrine, not preference.
  • No surveillance monetization. We do not collect behavioral data to sell to anyone.
  • 0% platform fee on the creator-economy contract (/creator-economy.json, schema p31.creatorEconomy/1.0.0).
  • 30-day notice before any change to the fee or revenue contract — published on this page and the creator-economy contract.
  • Refunds available within 30 days of donation for any reason. Email the operator (channel below).
  • Anonymous giving supported via Ko-fi without account creation; recurring giving requires the donor's chosen platform login.

IRS Form 990 publication

Once the IRS issues our 501(c)(3) determination, we will file Form 990 (or 990-EZ / 990-N depending on annual gross receipts) for each fiscal year. Filed copies will appear here as PDF downloads with cover-page summaries, and will also be available on ProPublica's Nonprofit Explorer through the IRS public-data feed.

Status: Awaiting IRS determination letter
Earliest 990: FY 2026 (covering April 3, 2026 onward)
Publication target: Within 30 days of filing
Format: Filed PDF + plain-language cover summary

Report a financial concern

If you suspect funds are being mishandled, the receipt language is incorrect, or there is any other concern about how P31 Labs is using donor money, please email the operator at will@p31ca.org with the subject [financial-concern]. The Code of Conduct (/code-of-conduct) protects good-faith reporters.

If you would like to support this work

We do not run urgency loops or guilt copy. The work continues whether you give or not. If giving fits your budget and you trust the public record on this page, here are the channels:

  • Ko-fi: ko-fi.com/p31labs — one-time or monthly, no account required for one-time
  • Stripe Checkout: via donate — receipts include IRS-conditional language until the determination letter arrives
  • Stock or planned giving: contact the operator at will@p31ca.org
