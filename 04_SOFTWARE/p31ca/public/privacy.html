What this site is

p31ca.org hosts static HTML, JSON contracts, and client-side tools (e.g. onboarding, mesh landing pages). Pages may use browser localStorage or sessionStorage for UX (dial state, onboarding progress). You can clear site data in your browser settings.

Edge & Workers

When you use features that call our Cloudflare Workers (for example personal mesh k4-personal), requests go to the URLs published in our public constants and health checks. Those services have their own storage (e.g. Durable Objects / KV) described in their architecture docs. We do not sell personal data; creator-economy terms are public at /creator-economy.json.

Analytics

We do not rely on third-party advertising trackers on these static deliverables. Infrastructure providers (e.g. CDN / host) may log technical metadata (IP, user agent) under their own policies.
