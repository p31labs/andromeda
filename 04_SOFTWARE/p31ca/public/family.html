Quantum Family

Neurodivergent Kids Hub

01. What It Is

Quantum Family is a tactile PWA built for neurodivergent kids — specifically Bash (10) and Willow. It runs on Android tablets, uses touch-first input, and loads in a warm cream palette designed to be calming rather than stimulating.

Every feature has a defined endpoint. No infinite scroll, no dopamine loops, no failure states. The app is a quiet tool, not a game that demands attention.

02. Eight Sections

  • ▸
    Home — Daily Anchor Greeting, date, and a soft daily affirmation. The stable starting point for every session.
  • ▸
    Breathe — Regulation Engine Box breathing (4-4-4-4) and calm breathing (4-7-8). Animated breathing ring with phase labels. Tap to start, tap to stop. No auto-loop.
  • ▸
    Ground — 5-4-3-2-1 Guided sensory grounding exercise. Five things you can see, four you can touch, three you can hear, two you can smell, one you can taste. Tap to advance each step.
  • ▸
    Sounds — Sound Garden Eight tactile sound pads. Web Audio synthesis — no samples. Each pad has a distinct timbre. Touch-responsive with visual feedback.
  • ▸
    Draw — Free Canvas Full-viewport drawing canvas. Color picker, brush size, eraser, clear. Pointer events — works with finger, stylus, or mouse. No save pressure.
  • ▸
    Family — Posner Molecule Three.js Ca₉(PO₄)₆ Posner molecule visualizer. Drag to rotate. Explode/spin mode toggle. A shared visual anchor for the family mesh concept.
  • ▸
    Calcium — Ca²⁺ Tracker Kid-friendly calcium intake log. Visual progress toward daily goal. Tied to the P31 Ca²⁺ protocol baseline — same science, simpler interface.
  • ▸
    Love Notes — Notes Jar A jar of eight love notes from Dad. Tap to read one. Gentle fade animation. Fixed set — they don't expire or rotate out.

No Failure States
Every interaction completes. No timers, no scores, no streaks.
