## Troubleshooting (When Things Don't Work)

### Problem: "Can't find the dashboard"
**Solution:** 
- Check the web address is exactly: `https://command-center.trimtab-signal.workers.dev`
- Make sure you're connected to the internet (Wi-Fi or cell data)

### Problem: "Triangle won't turn green"
**Solution:**
- Wait 2 minutes
- Refresh the page (pull down on phone, or press F5 on computer)
- Try Step 2 again
