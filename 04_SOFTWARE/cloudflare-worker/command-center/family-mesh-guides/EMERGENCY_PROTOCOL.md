# 🚨 Family Mesh Emergency Protocol
## What to Do When Things Go Wrong

---

## 🟢 LEVEL 1: No Problem (Normal Operation)

**Status:** All green triangles, everyone chatting

**What to Do:**
- ✅ Keep chatting and sharing
- ✅ Have fun together!
- ✅ Check in on each other

**Check Every:** 30 minutes (just to say hi!)

---

## 🟡 LEVEL 2: Minor Issue (Someone Can't Connect)

**Status:** One or two yellow or red dots

### Step 1: Wait
- Wait 2-5 minutes
- Sometimes the internet is just slow
- Try refreshing the page (pull down on phone)

### Step 2: Check Basics
- Is your phone/computer connected to the internet?
- Can you open other websites?
- Is your battery charged?

### Step 3: Try Again
- Close the browser/app
- Open it again
- Click your name again

### Step 4: Call for Help
If still not working after 5 minutes:
- **Call Dad:** 912-227-4980
- **Call Mom:** (Mom's number)
- **Text:** "Mesh not working, can you help?"

**What NOT to Do:**
- ❌ Don't panic
- ❌ Don't keep trying the same thing 100 times
- ❌ Don't get frustrated

---

## 🔴 LEVEL 3: MAJOR PROBLEM (No One Can Connect)

**Status:** All red, or website won't load at all

### IMMEDIATE ACTIONS:

1. **Stay Calm**
   - Take 3 deep breaths
   - Remember: phones still work!

2. **Check If It's Just You**
   - Can Mom connect? (Call her)
   - Can S.J. connect? (Call him)
   - If they can, it's just your device

3. **If NO ONE Can Connect:**
   - This might be a bigger problem
   - Switch to phone calls
   - Use regular texting
   - **Call Dad immediately: 912-227-4980**

4. **If It's an Emergency:**
   - **CALL 911** (always works)
   - Don't wait for the mesh
   - Don't try to fix it yourself
   - **911 is for real emergencies only**

### What to Tell Dad:
- "The mesh is all red"
- "No one can connect"
- "We're using phones instead"
- "Is this a big problem or small problem?"

---

## 🚨 TRUE EMERGENCY (Call 911)

### When to Call 911:
- 🚑 Someone is hurt
- 🔥 There's a fire
- 🚨 Someone is in danger
- 🆘 You feel scared for someone's safety
- 🏥 Medical emergency (heart attack, stroke, severe injury)

### What to Say:
1. "My address is: [YOUR ADDRESS]"
2. "My phone number is: [YOUR NUMBER]"
3. "This is what's happening: [DESCRIBE]
4. "How many people are hurt: [NUMBER]"
5. "I need help right now"

### Stay on the Line:
- Don't hang up first
- Answer their questions
- Follow their instructions
- Help is coming

## 🎯 Golden Rules

### 1. **Phones > Mesh**
   - Regular phones always work
   - Mesh is just a bonus
   - If mesh breaks, use phones

### 2. **Don't Panic**
   - Most problems fix themselves
   - Wait 5 minutes before calling for help
   - Take deep breaths

### 3. **Ask for Help**
   - It's okay to not know
   - Dad is there to help
   - No question is silly

### 4. **Safety First**
   - 911 for real emergencies
   - Don't try to be a hero
   - Get to safety first, fix tech later

### 5. **Stay Connected**
   - Even if mesh is broken
   - Use phones, texts, calls
   - Check in on each other

## 📱 Practice Drill (Do This Together!)

### Once a Month:
1. **Pretend the mesh is broken**
2. **Try to connect for 5 minutes**
3. **When it doesn't work, call each other**
4. **Say: "Mesh is broken, using phones instead"**
5. **Problem solved!**

This way, everyone knows what to do when it happens for real.
