## EXHIBIT WRJ-013: NOTICE OF SIGNAL CORRECTION

### Notice of Clerical Error in Medical Records and Request for Judicial Notice

---

## I. IDENTIFICATION OF SIGNAL CORRUPTION

This notice serves to correct a clerical error appearing in certain medical records produced in response to Plaintiff's Request No. 7, and to request judicial notice of the correction pursuant to O.C.G.A. § 24-2-201.

**A. The Error:**

The medical record(s) from certain healthcare providers incorrectly record the Defendant's condition as "Hyperparathyroidism" (excessive parathyroid hormone) when in fact the Defendant suffers from **Hypoparathyroidism** (deficient parathyroid hormone).

**B. The Correct Diagnosis:**

- **ICD-10 Code:** E20.9 — Hypoparathyroidism, unspecified
- **Full Name:** Chronic Hypoparathyroidism
- **Onset:** 2003 (following seizure event at age 18)

---

## II. FORENSIC CORRECTION

The Defendant has included this notice to ensure the evidentiary record accurately reflects his medical condition:

| Field | Erroneous Entry | Corrected Entry |

| ICD-10 Code | E21.x (unspecified) | **E20.9** |
| Pathophysiology | Excess PTH | **Deficient PTH** |

---

## III. OPERATIONAL IMPACT

This clerical error has material implications for the Defendant's ability to function in this litigation:

**A. Cognitive Prosthetic Necessity:**

The Defendant's hypoparathyroidism causes documented cognitive impairment including:
- Difficulty with high-volume information processing
- Executive function deficits
- Memory inconsistencies during calcium fluctuations

These symptoms are distinct from and exacerbated by the Defendant's AuDHD diagnosis. The use of AI tools (large language models) constitutes a **necessary cognitive prosthetic** to compensate for the biological deficits caused by hypoparathyroidism.

**B. The "Broken Hardware" Defense:**

The Defendant's metabolic condition represents a form of "biological hardware failure" that impairs his capacity for certain administrative tasks. Just as a litigant with a visual impairment requires assistive technology to read documents, the Defendant requires AI-assisted processing to compensate for his metabolic-induced cognitive deficits.

---

## IV. TACTICAL PURPOSE: PROOF OF UNRELIABILITY OF EXTERNAL MEDICAL RECORDS

This correction demonstrates the unreliability of external medical records and supports the Defendant's position that:

1. **Source Verification Required:** All medical records must be verified against primary source documentation (laboratory results, diagnostic codes) rather than assumed accurate.

2. **Pattern of Signal Corruption:** The Defendant reserves the right to identify additional clerical errors in medical records produced by third-party providers.

3. **Self-Certification of Diagnosis:** The Defendant's production of laboratory results demonstrating his calcium levels provides the most reliable evidence of his actual condition, independent of potentially corrupted external records.

---
