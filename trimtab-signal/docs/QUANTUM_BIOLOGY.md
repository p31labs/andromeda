## The Posner Molecule: Quantum Information Storage

The Posner molecule (Ca<sub>9</sub>(PO<sub>4</sub>)<sub>6</sub>) is central to our quantum biological research. This cluster of calcium and phosphate ions may serve as a natural quantum information storage system in biological systems.

### Quantum Properties

- **Nuclear Spin States:** Phosphorus-31 nuclei can maintain quantum coherence
- **Entanglement:** Multiple Posner molecules can become quantum entangled
- **Decoherence Protection:** The molecular structure may protect against environmental noise
- **Biological Integration:** Naturally occurring in bone and cellular processes

### Research Applications

**Cognitive Function:** Theoretical models suggest Posner molecules may play a role in memory and consciousness.

**Quantum Computing:** Biological systems may provide insights into room-temperature quantum computing.

**Medical Applications:** Understanding quantum effects in biology could lead to new medical treatments.
