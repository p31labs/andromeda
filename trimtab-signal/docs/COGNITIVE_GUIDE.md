# 🥄 The Cognitive Guide: Preserving Bandwidth

**Author:** Will Johnson  
**Subject:** The Dual-Ledger Economy

## The Problem with Open Source

Traditional open-source ecosystems incentivize burnout. "Green squares" on a contribution graph become a measure of self-worth, leading developers—especially neurodivergent developers who may experience hyper-fixation—to work until they crash.

## The Solution: Spoons & Karma

In the Phosphorus31 ecosystem, we have decoupled "reputation" from "energy".

### Spoons (Energy/Capacity)

Every node starts the day with 5 Spoons.

Interacting with the system (assembling molecules, decrypting hashes, pushing PRs) costs Spoons.

When you hit 0 Spoons, the Oracle bot locks you out. This is not a punishment. It is a boundary. The system is telling you to rest.

**Spoons regenerate at midnight UTC.**

### Karma (Reputation/Resonance)

Earned by verifying academic hashes (Tetrahedron Protocol) or contributing to stable Posner molecules.

**Karma is permanent.** It determines your Node Tier (e.g., Observer -> Phosphorus Node).

## Directive: Protect Your Spoons

Build the network, but do not let the network consume you.

### Neurodivergent Considerations

- **Hyper-fixation Management:** The Spoons system prevents endless loops of engagement
- **Energy Budgeting:** Clear limits help with executive function and planning
- **Burnout Prevention:** Automatic enforcement protects against overwork
- **Sustainable Contribution:** Long-term engagement over short-term intensity

### Best Practices

1. **Track Your Spoons:** Monitor your energy levels throughout the day
2. **Prioritize High-Impact Actions:** Focus on contributions that matter most
3. **Respect Your Limits:** Don't push past 0 Spoons
4. **Rest is Productive:** Recovery time is essential for sustained contribution
5. **Community Support:** Help others manage their Spoons and recognize when they need to step back

### Node Tiers & Spoons

- **Observer (0 Karma):** 5 Spoons/day, basic participation
- **Contributor (10+ Karma):** 6 Spoons/day, enhanced privileges
- **Phosphorus Node (50+ Karma):** 7 Spoons/day, community leadership
- **Quantum Architect (100+ Karma):** 8 Spoons/day, system governance

## The Philosophy

This system recognizes that human energy is finite and valuable. By protecting individual capacity, we ensure the long-term health and sustainability of the entire network.

**Remember:** A burned-out contributor helps no one. A rested, energized contributor can change the world.
