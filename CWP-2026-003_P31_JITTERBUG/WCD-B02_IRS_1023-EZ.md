**PRIMARY ACTIVITIES:**

**Activity 1: Open-Source Assistive Technology Development (40% of activities)**

- **BONDING:** A molecular chemistry game that facilitates parent-child connection across distance, with timestamped engagement records for clinical and legal documentation.
- **The Buffer:** A communication processing tool that detects fawning patterns (a trauma response) and provides single, transparent recommendations. Pre-market communication support software. No FDA classification claimed.
- **Spaceship Earth:** A comprehensive dashboard mapping cognitive architectures and providing real-time metrics for neurodivergent self-regulation.

These tools are distributed at no cost under open-source licenses, ensuring accessibility regardless of economic status.

**Activity 2: Educational Programs (30% of activities)**
P31 Labs provides free educational content and community programs in:

Programs are delivered through our Discord community, public GitHub repositories, and published research papers deposited in Zenodo (DOI-indexed).

**Activity 3: Housing Stability Support (20% of activities)**
P31 Labs provides targeted support for neurodivergent individuals experiencing or at risk of homelessness, including:
- Technology-enabled housing stability monitoring
- Connection to local rapid re-housing resources
- Integration with Emergency Solutions Grants (ESG) programs
- Peer support through community networks

**Activity 4: Scientific Research (10% of activities)**
P31 Labs conducts and publishes research into:
