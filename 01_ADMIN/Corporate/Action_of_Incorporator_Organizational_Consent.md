# ACTION BY UNANIMOUS WRITTEN CONSENT IN LIEU OF

## FIRST ORGANIZATIONAL MEETING OF THE BOARD OF DIRECTORS

## P31 LABS, INC.

**Date:** April 11, 2026

The undersigned, being all of the Directors of **P31 Labs, Inc.**, a Georgia domestic nonprofit corporation (the "Corporation"), acting pursuant to O.C.G.A. § 14-3-821, do hereby unanimously adopt the following resolutions:

---

## 1. ARTICLES OF INCORPORATION

**RESOLVED,** that the Articles of Incorporation filed with the Georgia Secretary of State on April 3, 2026, are hereby ratified, approved, and accepted.

---

## 2. ADOPTION OF BYLAWS

**RESOLVED,** that the Bylaws of the Corporation, providing for a 72-hour ADA review period and unanimous consent for asset transfer, are hereby adopted as the Bylaws of the Corporation, and the Secretary is directed to insert a copy of the Bylaws in the corporate minute book.

---

## 3. ELECTION OF OFFICERS

**RESOLVED,** that the following persons are elected to the offices set forth opposite their names, to serve until their successors are duly elected and qualified:

| Office | Name |
|--------|------|
| President & Chief Executive Officer | William R. Johnson |
| Secretary | William R. Johnson |
| Treasurer | William R. Johnson |

---

## 4. ADOPTION OF CONFLICT OF INTEREST POLICY

**RESOLVED,** that the IRS Model Conflict of Interest Policy, including the specific Personal Legal Proceedings Provision prohibiting the use of corporate funds for the personal legal debts of any Director, is hereby adopted.

---

## 5. BANK ACCOUNT AUTHORIZATION

**RESOLVED,** that the President is authorized to open bank accounts on behalf of the Corporation at U.S. Bank, CrowdStreet, or a similar financial institution, and that the President shall be the authorized signatory on such accounts.

---

## 6. AUTHORIZATION FOR 501(c)(3) EXEMPTION

**RESOLVED,** that the President is authorized and directed to prepare and file IRS Form 1023-EZ to seek federal tax exemption under Section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code, and to pay the associated $275 filing fee.

---

## CERTIFICATION

This Consent may be executed in counterparts and shall have the same force and effect as a unanimous vote at a duly called meeting.
