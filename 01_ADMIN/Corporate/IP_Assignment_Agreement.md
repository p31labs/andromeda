# INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY ASSIGNMENT AGREEMENT

This Intellectual Property Assignment Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of **April 11, 2026** ("Effective Date"), by and between:

**ASSIGNOR:** William Rodger Johnson, an individual residing at 401 Powderhorn Road, Saint Marys, Georgia 31558 ("Assignor"); and

**ASSIGNEE:** P31 Labs, Inc., a Georgia Domestic Nonprofit Corporation, with its principal office at 401 Powderhorn Road, Saint Marys, Georgia 31558 ("Assignee" or "Organization").

---

## RECITALS

**A.** Assignor is the founder and a director of the Organization, which was incorporated on April 3, 2026, with the charitable mission of developing and distributing open-source assistive technology for neurodivergent individuals.

**B.** Prior to and following incorporation, Assignor developed certain software, firmware, documentation, research publications, and related intellectual property in furtherance of the Organization's charitable mission.

**C.** Assignor wishes to formally assign all such intellectual property to the Organization to ensure that it is held and used exclusively for the Organization's charitable purposes.

**D.** This assignment is made in consideration of Assignor's continued role as Founder and Director of the Organization, and in furtherance of the charitable mission for which the intellectual property was created.

---

## AGREEMENT

### 1. ASSIGNMENT OF INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

Assignor hereby irrevocably assigns, transfers, and conveys to Assignee all right, title, and interest in and to the following intellectual property (collectively, the "Assigned IP"):

**(a)** All software source code, object code, scripts, configuration files, build systems, and related technical documentation developed for or in connection with the P31 Labs ecosystem, including but not limited to: **BONDING, The Buffer, Spaceship Earth, Genesis Gate, Kenosis Mesh, Node Zero firmware, Node One firmware, TACTILE, CogPass**, and all applications deployed at p31ca.org and phosphorus31.org;

**(b)** All research papers, publications, preprints, and manuscripts published under the P31 Labs name or in furtherance of its mission, including publications deposited on Zenodo (DOIs: 10.5281/zenodo.18627420, 10.5281/zenodo.19411363, 10.5281/zenodo.19416491, 10.5281/zenodo.19432309, 10.5281/zenodo.19432313);

**(c)** All documentation including Work Control Documents (WCDs), Controlled Work Packages (CWPs), the Master Operations Manual, the GOD Ground Truth document, the WCD Registry, grant narratives, traction packages, and all operational documentation;

**(d)** All trademarks, service marks, logos, and branding assets associated with P31 Labs, including the K₄ tetrahedron mark, the ³¹P coral nucleus mark, the quantum branding kit, and the domain names p31ca.org, phosphorus31.org, and p31.io;

**(e)** All patent rights, including any provisional or non-provisional patent applications that may be filed for inventions developed in furtherance of the Organization's mission;

**(f)** All data, databases, telemetry logs, Genesis Block records, and analytics collected through the Organization's deployed systems;

**(g)** All derivative works, improvements, modifications, and extensions of any of the foregoing, whether now existing or hereafter created in furtherance of the Organization's mission.

### 2. CONSIDERATION

This assignment is made in consideration of:

**(a)** One Dollar ($1.00) and other good and valuable consideration, receipt of which is hereby acknowledged; and

**(b)** Assignor's continued involvement with the Organization as Founder and Director, which provides Assignor with the opportunity to further the charitable mission for which the intellectual property was created.

### 3. OPEN-SOURCE LICENSING

The Organization may, at its sole discretion, release any or all of the Assigned IP under open-source licenses consistent with its charitable mission. Such release does not diminish the Organization's ownership. BONDING is currently licensed under AGPL-3.0. The open-source licensing of the Assigned IP is integral to the Organization's charitable purpose of making assistive technology freely available to neurodivergent individuals.

### 4. REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES

Assignor represents and warrants that:

**(a)** Assignor is the sole creator of the Assigned IP or has the right to assign it;

**(b)** The Assigned IP was developed primarily after Assignor's separation from Department of Defense employment (approximately **September 30, 2025**) and was not developed using government resources or under government contract;

**(c)** The opposing party in the pending domestic relations proceeding has not asserted a claim of ownership over any of the Assigned IP and has expressly disclaimed interest in the physical property on which it resides;

**(d)** To Assignor's knowledge, the Assigned IP does not infringe the intellectual property rights of any third party.

### 5. FURTHER ASSURANCES

Assignor agrees to execute any additional documents and take any additional actions reasonably necessary to effectuate and perfect the assignment contemplated herein.

### 6. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Georgia.

### 7. NONPROFIT PURPOSE

This assignment is made in furtherance of the Organization's tax-exempt charitable purpose under Section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code. The Assigned IP shall be used exclusively for charitable, scientific, and educational purposes. No part of the Assigned IP shall inure to the private benefit of any individual.
