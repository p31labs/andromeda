## Key Legal Protections

1. **Asset Shield:** Any transfer/sale/encumbrance of corporate assets requires unanimous board consent
2. **Conflict of Interest:** No corporate funds for personal legal debts of directors
3. **Private Inurement:** Prohibition on scholarships/trusts benefiting director descendants
4. **IP Assignment:** All code/digital assets exclusively owned by P31 Labs, Inc.
5. **Property Contribution:** All development hardware contributed to nonprofit
6. **Patent Eligibility:** Ex parte Desjardins (2025) framework preserved
7. **Slander of Title:** O.C.G.A. § 51-9-11 extends to software IP
