# WCD-12: DUNA Charter Draft — Phenix Navigator DAO

**Entity:** Phenix Navigator DAO, LLC  
**Structure:** Wyoming Decentralized Unincorporated Nonprofit Association (DUNA)  
**Date:** March 31, 2026

---

## I. NAME AND PRINCIPAL OFFICE

1. **Name:** Phenix Navigator DAO, LLC
2. **Structure:** Decentralized Unincorporated Nonprofit Association (DUNA) under Wyoming law
3. **Principal Office:** State of Wyoming, County of Laramie (DAO-controlled; no physical address required)

---

## II. PURPOSE

The Phenix Navigator DAO is organized exclusively for charitable, scientific, and educational purposes within the meaning of Section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code, or corresponding section of any future federal tax law.

**Mission:** To develop and deploy open-source cognitive prosthetics, decentralized communication infrastructure, and neurodivergent advocacy tools that protect human cognitive autonomy from systemic institutional hostility.

---

## III. MEMBERSHIP AND GOVERNANCE

### A. Tiered Membership

| Tier | Role | Governance Weight |
|------|------|-------------------|
| **Founder Node** | Initial architects (William Johnson + AI agents) | Veto (only for emergency) |
| **Tetrahedron Node** | Core developers (max 4) | 3/4 consensus for major changes |
| **Mesh Node** | General community contributors | Simple majority for minor updates |

### B. Headless Governance

1. **No Central Executive:** The DAO is governed entirely by smart contract code.
2. **Immutability:** Core governance logic cannot be modified once deployed.
3. **Decentralized Execution:** All operational decisions execute via algorithmic consensus.

### C. Asset Custody (Delta Topology)

1. **Direct "Book" Holdings:** All assets held directly at transfer agents; no intermediaries.
2. **Multi-Sig Threshold:** Any single node can trigger transaction broadcast; 2-of-3 required for execution.
3. **No Seizable Assets:** All DAO assets are cryptographically held; incapable of being seized by legacy legal systems.

---

## IV. POWERS

1. To receive, hold, and manage funds for charitable purposes
2. To develop, license, and distribute open-source software
3. To enter into contracts with third parties for development services
4. To acquire real and personal property as necessary for the organization's mission
5. To sue and be sued in courts of competent jurisdiction

---

## V. DISSOLUTION

Upon dissolution of the DAO (triggered by smart contract vote with 4/4 Tetrahedron Node consensus):

1. All remaining assets shall be distributed to a 501(c)(3) organization with similar purposes
2. No member shall have any right to residual assets
3. The smart contract shall execute self-destruct, erasing all administrative access

---

## VI. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

1. **Open Source Default:** All software code is released under MIT/Apache 2.0 licenses.
2. **Defensive Publication:** All innovations are immediately published to Zenodo/Internet Archive as prior art.
3. **No Patents:** No patents shall be filed on core software innovations.

---

## VII. TAX STATUS

1. The Phenix Navigator DAO shall seek recognition as a 501(c)(3) tax-exempt organization.
2. All donations shall be tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law.
3. The DAO shall file annual Form 990-N with the IRS.
