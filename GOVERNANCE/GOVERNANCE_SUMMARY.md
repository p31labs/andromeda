## Asset Protection Strategy

### Personal Goodwill Framework
The entire governance framework is built around the concept of Personal Goodwill, which provides:

**Legal Foundation:**
- Georgia family law precedent (Miller v. Miller)
- Bankruptcy protection under 11 U.S.C. § 522(d)(9)
- Separate property classification
- Non-transferable asset status

**Technical Implementation:**
- Hardware Security Module (HSM) integration
- Cryptographic identity binding
- Biometric authentication requirements
- Device functionality tied to specific user

**Strategic Benefits:**
- Protection from creditor claims
- Exemption from divorce proceedings
- Bankruptcy asset protection
- Regulatory compliance maintenance
