### 1.1 "Contribution" means any original work of authorship, including any modifications or additions to an existing work, that is submitted by the Contributor to the Corporation for inclusion in, or documentation of, any of the Corporation's open-source projects. ### 1.2 "Project" means any open-source software project maintained by the Corporation, including but not limited to the Phenix Navigator system and related assistive technologies.

### 1.3 "Personal Goodwill" means the non-transferable intellectual property and technological developments that are inextricably bound to the identity of the Corporation's founder(s) and qualify as separate property under Georgia law.
