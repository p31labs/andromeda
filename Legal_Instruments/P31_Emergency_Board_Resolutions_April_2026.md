# UNANIMOUS WRITTEN CONSENT IN LIEU OF ORGANIZATIONAL MEETING

## OF THE BOARD OF DIRECTORS OF P31 LABS, INC.

**DATE:** April 11, 2026

The undersigned, constituting the entire Board of Directors of **P31 LABS, INC.**, a Georgia domestic nonprofit corporation (the "Corporation"), acting pursuant to O.C.G.A. § 14-3-821, hereby waive notice of a meeting and unanimously adopt the following emergency resolutions:

---

## RESOLUTION 1: CORPORATE ASSET SHIELD & UNANIMITY REQUIREMENT

**WHEREAS,** the Corporation's intellectual property, hardware prototypes (Node Zero), and codebase are critical public infrastructure intended for 501(c)(3) charitable purposes;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED,** pursuant to O.C.G.A. § 14-3-1202(b) and (d), that any transfer, sale, encumbrance, licensing, or liquidation of Corporate assets, code, or intellectual property shall require the unanimous written consent of the entire Board of Directors.

---

## RESOLUTION 2: FOUNDER-RELATED TRANSACTIONS & INDEPENDENCE

**WHEREAS,** the President and Founder, William R. Johnson, is currently undergoing contested personal legal proceedings;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED,** pursuant to O.C.G.A. § 14-3-862, that any transaction, legal stipulation, or court order attempting to categorize the Corporation's assets as the personal or marital property of the Founder shall be strictly contested by the Corporation. The Independent Director, **JOSEPH TYLER CISCO**, is hereby authorized to act as the sole signatory to defend the Corporation's assets against any such piercing of the corporate veil.

---

## RESOLUTION 3: IRS CONFLICT OF INTEREST POLICY ADOPTION

**RESOLVED,** that the Corporation hereby adopts the IRS Model Conflict of Interest Policy required for 501(c)(3) tax-exempt status, with the inclusion of the following specific **Personal Legal Proceedings Provision:**

*No corporate funds, liquidity, or Stripe/Ko-fi distributions shall be used to satisfy the personal legal debts, judgments, or family court obligations of any Director.*

---

## RESOLUTION 4: PROHIBITION OF PRIVATE INUREMENT

**RESOLVED,** that to comply strictly with Treasury Regulation § 1.501(c)(3)-1(d)(1)(ii), the Corporation is permanently prohibited from establishing any scholarship programs, asset transfers, or specialized financial trusts that specifically target or benefit the children or direct descendants of the Founder or any Board member.

---

## EXECUTION AND BINDING EFFECT

This Unanimous Written Consent may be executed in counterparts. The actions taken by this consent shall have the same force and effect as if taken at a duly called meeting of the Board of Directors.
