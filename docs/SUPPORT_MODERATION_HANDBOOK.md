## 🛡️ Support & Moderation Philosophy

### Core Principles
1. **Safety First**: Ensure a safe, welcoming environment for all community members
2. **Helpful & Constructive**: Provide helpful, solution-oriented support and moderation
3. **Consistent & Fair**: Apply rules and policies consistently and fairly to all members
4. **Educational Approach**: Use moderation as an opportunity for education and growth
5. **Community-Centric**: Prioritize community health and positive interactions

### Support Mission
To provide exceptional support that empowers users, resolves issues efficiently, and fosters a positive community experience while maintaining medical device compliance and standards.

### Moderation Mission
To maintain a safe, inclusive, and productive community environment through fair, consistent, and educational moderation practices that align with community values and medical device standards.

## 📋 Support Procedures & Guidelines

### Support Request Management

#### Ticket Intake & Triage
```yaml
Initial Assessment:
  - Greet user and acknowledge their concern
  - Categorize issue type (technical, feature request, general inquiry)
  - Assess urgency and impact level
  - Determine appropriate support tier

Categorization System:
  - Technical Issues: Bug reports, errors, functionality problems
  - Feature Requests: New features or enhancements
  - General Inquiries: Questions about usage or capabilities
  - Account Issues: Login, permissions, access problems
  - Community Issues: Moderation, conflicts, policy questions

Urgency Levels:
  - Critical: System-wide outage or security issue (immediate response)
  - High: Major functionality broken for multiple users (2-hour response)
  - Medium: Individual user issue with workaround available (24-hour response)
  - Low: Minor issue or enhancement request (72-hour response)
```

#### Support Response Templates
```yaml
Initial Response Template:
  "Hello [User]! Thank you for reaching out to us. I'm [Name] from the P31 Andromeda support team. I understand this is important to you, and I'm here to help. Let me look into this for you right away."

Technical Issue Response:
  "I understand this issue is frustrating. Let me gather some information to help diagnose the problem. Could you please provide: 1) The exact error message you're seeing, 2) What you were doing when the issue occurred, 3) Your device/browser information, and 4) Any steps you've already tried?"

Feature Request Response:
  "Thank you for your feature suggestion! We appreciate community input in shaping P31 Andromeda. I've logged your request for review by our development team. While I can't promise when or if this will be implemented, all suggestions are carefully considered based on community feedback and technical feasibility."
```

#### Escalation Procedures
```yaml
When to Escalate:
  - Issue exceeds support team's technical knowledge
  - Multiple users reporting the same problem
  - Security or compliance concerns
  - User requests speak to development team
  - Issue impacts core functionality

Escalation Process:
  1. Document all troubleshooting steps taken
  2. Gather all relevant information and user details
  3. Complete escalation form with clear description
  4. Notify user of escalation and expected timeline
  5. Follow up to ensure issue is being addressed

Escalation Communication:
  "I've escalated your issue to our technical team as it requires specialized knowledge. They'll be in touch within [timeframe]. In the meantime, here's what you can expect: [details about process and communication]."
```

### Common Support Scenarios

#### Technical Troubleshooting
```yaml
Login Issues:
  - Verify account status and permissions
  - Check for system maintenance or outages
  - Guide through password reset process
  - Test with different browsers/devices
  - Escalate if issue persists

Feature Not Working:
  - Verify user has correct permissions
  - Check feature availability in user's plan
  - Guide through troubleshooting steps
  - Document steps for escalation if needed
  - Provide workarounds if available

Performance Issues:
  - Gather system information (browser, device, internet)
  - Check for known performance issues
  - Suggest optimization steps
  - Monitor for patterns across multiple users
  - Escalate if widespread issue suspected
```

#### Community Support
```yaml
Conflict Resolution:
  - Listen to all parties involved
  - Remain neutral and objective
  - Focus on behavior, not personalities
  - Suggest compromise or alternative solutions
  - Escalate to moderation team if needed

New User Assistance:
  - Provide comprehensive onboarding information
  - Suggest relevant resources and tutorials
  - Connect with community mentors if available
  - Follow up to ensure successful integration
  - Address any confusion or concerns promptly

Feedback and Suggestions:
  - Thank user for their input
  - Explain how feedback is collected and reviewed
  - Set appropriate expectations for response
  - Document suggestions for team review
  - Follow up if additional information needed
```

### Support Quality Standards

#### Communication Guidelines
```yaml
Tone and Language:
  - Use clear, professional, and friendly language
  - Avoid technical jargon unless user is technical
  - Be empathetic and understanding
  - Maintain patience, especially with frustrated users
  - Use positive language and focus on solutions

Response Quality:
  - Provide complete and accurate information
  - Double-check links and resources before sharing
  - Follow up to ensure issue is resolved
  - Document solutions for future reference
  - Escalate when appropriate rather than providing incorrect information

Professional Boundaries:
  - Maintain professional distance while being helpful
  - Avoid personal opinions or unrelated discussions
  - Don't make promises you can't keep
  - Refer to appropriate resources for non-support issues
  - Know when to end conversations that aren't productive
```

#### Resolution Standards
```yaml
First Contact Resolution:
  - Aim to resolve issues in first interaction when possible
  - Provide comprehensive answers that prevent follow-up questions
  - Offer additional resources for self-help
  - Clearly communicate next steps if resolution requires time

Follow-up Procedures:
  - Check back with users after resolution
  - Ensure solution is working as expected
  - Ask if additional assistance is needed
  - Document user satisfaction and feedback
  - Update knowledge base with new solutions

Documentation Standards:
  - Record all interactions in support system
  - Document troubleshooting steps and solutions
  - Note any patterns or recurring issues
  - Update knowledge base articles as needed
  - Share insights with team for process improvement
```

## ⚖️ Moderation Policies & Procedures

### Community Guidelines Enforcement

#### Prohibited Content & Behavior
```yaml
Harassment and Bullying:
  - Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments
  - Targeted harassment of individuals or groups
  - Cyberbullying or intimidation tactics
  - Doxxing or sharing private information without consent
  - Threats of violence or harm

Spam and Self-Promotion:
  - Excessive self-promotion or advertising
  - Repetitive posting of the same content
  - Bots or automated posting without permission
  - Pyramid schemes or get-rich-quick schemes
  - Off-topic promotional content

Inappropriate Content:
  - NSFW or adult content
  - Violent or graphic content
  - Hate speech or discriminatory content
  - Illegal content or instructions for illegal activities
  - Medical misinformation or dangerous advice

Disruptive Behavior:
  - Trolling or intentionally provoking others
  - Flame wars or inciting arguments
  - Impersonation of staff or other users
  - Circumventing moderation or bans
  - Excessive arguing with moderation decisions
```

#### Warning System & Escalation
```yaml
Level 1 - Verbal Warning:
  - First offense for minor rule violations
  - Private message explaining the issue
  - Clear explanation of expected behavior
  - No public record or impact on account
  - Focus on education and correction

Level 2 - Written Warning:
  - Repeated or more serious violations
  - Public warning in appropriate channel
  - Detailed explanation of violation
  - Clear consequences for continued behavior
  - Logged in moderation system

Level 3 - Temporary Mute:
  - Continued violations or disruptive behavior
  - 1 hour to 24 hours duration
  - Prevents user from sending messages
  - Allows user to read and reflect
  - Opportunity to cool down and reconsider behavior

Level 4 - Temporary Ban:
  - Serious violations or repeated offenses
  - 1 day to 7 days duration
  - Complete removal from server
  - Opportunity for reflection and improvement
  - Requires appeal for reinstatement

Level 5 - Permanent Ban:
  - Severe violations or irreparable damage
  - Hate speech, threats, or extreme harassment
  - Repeated serious offenses after warnings
  - Protection of community safety and well-being
  - Final decision with limited appeal options
```

#### Moderation Decision Framework
```yaml
Factors to Consider:
  - Severity of the violation
  - User's history and previous warnings
  - Intent vs. accidental behavior
  - Impact on other community members
  - Potential for education and improvement
  - Precedent and consistency with past decisions

Decision Process:
  1. Assess the situation and gather all relevant information
  2. Review user's history and previous interactions
  3. Consider the impact on the community
  4. Determine appropriate action based on guidelines
  5. Document the decision and reasoning
  6. Communicate the decision to the user

Consistency Checks:
  - Compare with similar past cases
  - Consult with other moderators for serious cases
  - Ensure decision aligns with community values
  - Consider long-term impact on community health
```

#### Medical Device Compliance Issues
```yaml
Content Review:
  - Monitor for medical misinformation
  - Address inappropriate medical advice
  - Handle discussions about medical device usage
  - Ensure compliance with medical device regulations
  - Coordinate with compliance team for complex issues

Response Protocol:
  - Address misinformation promptly and accurately
  - Redirect medical questions to appropriate professionals
  - Provide accurate information about device capabilities
  - Escalate compliance concerns to appropriate team
  - Document issues for compliance tracking

Educational Approach:
  - Use moderation as opportunity for education
  - Provide accurate information and resources
  - Explain why certain content is problematic
  - Encourage responsible discussion
  - Foster informed and respectful community dialogue
```

#### Conflict Resolution
```yaml
De-escalation Techniques:
  - Remain calm and professional
  - Acknowledge concerns without taking sides
  - Focus on behavior rather than personalities
  - Suggest taking conversation to private messages
  - Offer to mediate if appropriate

Mediation Process:
  1. Separate parties if needed
  2. Listen to each party's perspective
  3. Identify the root cause of conflict
  4. Suggest compromise or alternative solutions
  5. Follow up to ensure resolution

When to Escalate:
  - Threats of violence or harm
  - Harassment that cannot be resolved
  - Repeated conflicts between same individuals
  - Issues that impact broader community
  - Situations requiring administrative intervention
```

#### Crisis Management
```yaml
Crisis Identification:
  - Multiple users reporting same issue
  - Sudden influx of negative feedback
  - Security breaches or data concerns
  - Major technical outages
  - External attacks or coordinated harassment

Crisis Response:
  1. Assess scope and impact of the situation
  2. Activate crisis response team
  3. Implement immediate containment measures
  4. Communicate transparently with community
  5. Coordinate with development and support teams
  6. Monitor situation and adjust response as needed

Communication During Crisis:
  - Acknowledge the issue promptly
  - Provide regular updates on progress
  - Be honest about what is known and unknown
  - Avoid speculation or promises that can't be kept
  - Thank community for patience and understanding
```
